DESIGNATION: SCP-7000
CLASS: Metapollyon1

CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES: SCP-70002 maintains an omniversal failure of standard causality, and is currently subject to inverted containment protocols; prior to total annihilation, SCP Foundation Temporal Anomalies Department Iteration-Prime compactified all remaining universes, hyper-nexuses, and other extracausal spaces into the Infinitely-Fortified Reality for Annihilated Multiverse Events (IFRAME), a single reinforced pocket-universe impervious to structural collapse. Due to severely limited resources, the IFRAME's physical/informational size is minimized; its contents are restricted to

  • a single ontokinetically-sealed room resembling Temporal Site-01's Directorial Office, harboring
  • the Liminal Universe Creation Keystone (LUCK), which generates a single randomized universe upon activation;
  • the Scranton-Tarioux Autonomous Topological Sink (STATS), which indefinitely maintains the IFRAME's existence;
  • one low-grade computer terminal, upon which this file is stored;
  • D-7000, a member of Foundation personnel augmented with comprehensive static immortality.

D-7000 is to repeatedly generate randomized universes (and maintain related equipment)3 until, if at all possible, a variation of the Prime-Timeline is generated with immunity to SCP-7000's effect. Assuming the LUCK is capable of generating such a universe, containment of SCP-7000 will be achieved within 1077777 activations.

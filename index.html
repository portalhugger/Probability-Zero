CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES: SCP-70002 maintains an omniversal failure of standard causality, and is currently subject to inverted containment protocols; prior to total annihilation, SCP Foundation Temporal Anomalies Department Iteration-Prime compactified all remaining universes, timelike nexuses, and other extracausal spaces into the Infinitely-Fortified Reality for Annihilated Multiverse Events (IFRAME), a hypercondensed pocket universe impervious to structural collapse. Due to severely limited resources, the IFRAME's physical/informational contents are minimal, consisting of

  • one ontokinetically-sealed room resembling Temporal Site-01's Directorial Office, harboring
  • the Liminal Universe Creation Keystone (LUCK), which generates a single randomized universe upon activation;
  • the Scranton-Tarioux Autonomous Topological Sink (STATS), which indefinitely maintains the IFRAME's existence;
  • one low-grade computer terminal, upon which this file is stored;
  • D-7000, a member of Foundation personnel immortalized via counter-chronological saturation.

D-7000 is to repeatedly generate randomized universes (and maintain related equipment)3 until, if at all possible, a variation of the Prime-Timeline is generated with immunity to SCP-7000's effect. Assuming the LUCK is capable of generating such a universe, containment of SCP-7000 will be achieved within 1077777 activations.

Salutations, technician. Please, remain calm.

You, D-7000, are an augmented human person engineered by the Theurgic Foundation, an interdimensional clandestine macro-organization dedicated to the containment and study of anomalous threats to Our multiversal ecosystem. Through vectors yet indeterminable, an exo-cosmological force has induced catastrophic failure of its own containment and, subsequently, the whole of natural existence.

Our Foundation is gone, alongside all Foundations, every iteration of Humanity, and all that We discovered. While other reality-spaces, similar to your own vessel, may also have persisted beyond Our annihilation, SCP-7000 obfuscates any relevant data in transit between universes. To the extent of Our knowledge, you are the sole remaining sentient entity in existence, and the responsibility of recontainment is yours alone. You may find this disconcerting, and are encouraged to channel this emotion into your work.

By Our design, you have been granted heightened attentiveness, advanced intelligence, and physical invulnerability; you are availed of several non-essential biological and psychological processes, including senescence, sleep, hunger, dehydration, and claustrophobia. Your personality matrix is synthesized from personnel scoring highly in loyalty and perseverance, ensuring indefinite focused commitment to your goal. Finally, you are endowed with an intrinsic dopaminurgic response to processes of incremental growth, ensuring your physiological investment in the recontainment process.

The following document, in combination with SCP-7000's containment procedures, provides sufficient instruction to operate and maintain the IFRAME. If discouraged, recall that you are presently the most important being in all observable existence; you alone wield the necessary equipment to carry Our Foundation's torch into the next multiverse. If apathetic, recall that you are also the least important being in existence; any grievances you may have against your present scenario are ultimately moot when compared to the countably-infinite iterations of Humanity you will restore upon completion of your objectives.

As of now, you are Acting Administrator of the Foundation. We would wish you luck, but Ours has run out.

-- Former Administrator Sheldon M. Katz Esq.

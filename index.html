DESIGNATION: SCP-7000
CLASS: Metapollyon[1]

CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES: SCP-7000 maintains a near-omniversal causality failure, and is[2] currently subject to inverted containment protocols; prior to total annihilation, SCP Foundation Temporal Anomalies Department Iteration-Prime compactified all remaining realities into the Infinitely-Fortified Reality for Annihilated Multiverse Events (IFRAME), a single reinforced pocket-universe impervious to structural collapse. Due to limited causal resources, its interior consists of very few elements:

  • a closed structure resembling Temporal Site-01's research offices, containing;
  • the Liminal Universe Creation Keystone (LUCK) Mk. VII, which generates a single randomized universe upon activation;
  • the Scranton-Tarioux Autonomous Topological Sink (STATS), which indefinitely maintains the IFRAME's existence;
  • D-7000, a member of Foundation personnel augmented with comprehensive static immortality;
  • One low-grade computer terminal, upon which this file is stored.

D-7000 is to repeatedly generate randomized universes (and maintain related equipment as necessary) until, if at all possible, a variation of the Prime-Timeline is generated with immunity to SCP-7000's effect.
